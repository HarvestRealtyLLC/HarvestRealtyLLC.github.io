Move-Out Timeline

Step 1

Submit Written Notice (30-60 Days Before)

Provide written notice of your intent to vacate. Check your lease for the required notice period (typically 30-60 days).

Step 2

Schedule Move-Out Inspection (2 Weeks Before)

Contact us to schedule a pre-move-out inspection. This helps identify any items that need attention before your final walkthrough.

Step 3

Clean & Repair (1 Week Before)

Deep clean the property and address any damages beyond normal wear and tear. Refer to the checklist below.

Step 4

Final Walkthrough (Move-Out Day)

Complete the final inspection with our team. Return all keys, garage remotes, and access devices.

Step 5

Security Deposit Return (Within 30 Days)

Your security deposit will be returned within 30 days, minus any deductions for damages or unpaid rent.
